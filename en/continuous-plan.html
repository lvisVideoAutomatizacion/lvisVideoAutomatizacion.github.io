Monthly support in multimedia accessibility

An expert team supports you
to apply accessibility to all your audiovisual content,
integrating into your workflow and ensuring
that both new and existing productions comply with inclusive standards.

  • Plan adapted to the real production workload starting from one hour per month.
  • Continuous accessibility production: Unused minutes in one month roll over to the following months.
  • Quality assurance: We comply with accessibility standards (WCAG and UNE) and other project-specific requirements in a responsible way.
  • Focus on creating new content: Avoid delays or accessibility errors that are more costly to fix later.

How we work

  1. At the beginning of the month we receive a set of videos to make accessible, prioritized by your company.
  2. During the month we work on your content, delivering completed collections as we progress.
  3. You have up to three months after delivery to review and request corrections.
  4. If in a given month you don’t have the full volume of videos available, the balance is carried over to future months.
