Continuous Plan

Advance every month with a team of multimedia accessibility experts
in your audiovisual production, valid for existing media and new creations


Accessibility is not a one-time project: it is a process.
We accompany you month by month to apply real improvements to your audiovisual production,
delivering tangible results by enhancing both existing and new creations.

Monthly multimedia accessibility support

A team of experts guides you
to apply accessibility to all your audiovisual content,
integrating into your workflow and ensuring
that both new and already produced content comply with inclusive standards.

  • • Plan tailored to the actual production workload starting from one hour per month.
  • • Continuous accessibility production: Unused minutes in one month are carried over to future months.
  • • Quality assurance: We comply with accessibility standards (WCAG and UNE) and other project-specific requirements responsibly.
  • • Focus on creating new content: Avoid delays or accessibility errors that are more costly to fix later.

How we work

  • • At the start of the month, we receive a prioritized volume of videos from your company to make accessible.
  • • Throughout the month we work on your content, delivering completed collections as we progress.
  • • You have up to three months after delivery to review and request corrections.
  • • If one month you don’t have the full video volume available, it is carried over to future months.
