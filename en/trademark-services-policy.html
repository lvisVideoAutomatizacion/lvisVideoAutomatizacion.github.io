LViS Brand and Product Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2024.
At LViS, we value the integrity and recognition of our brands, products, and services. This policy aims to regulate the use of our brands, logos, trade names, and any other related assets, as well as to establish guidelines for the correct representation of our products.

1. Brand Ownership

All rights to the brands, logos, trade names, designs, and other identifying elements of LViS are the exclusive property of LViS Vídeo Automatización S.L. or its licensors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • The names "LViS Studio", "LViS Me", "LViS Starter", "LViS Actions Builder", "LViS Videogram Builder", and any other trade name associated with the platform.
  • Logos, slogans, brand imagery, icons, and graphic elements.

2. Permitted Use of the Brands

The use of our brands is permitted only in the following cases:

  • Registered customers: To promote that they use our services, always respecting the established brand guidelines.
  • Authorized partners: Through a written agreement that allows the specific use of our brands.
  • Informational mentions: To cite or refer to LViS in articles, publications, or non-commercial content, provided it is done accurately and not misleadingly.

3. Prohibited Use of the Brands

The use of our brands is not permitted in the following cases:

  • To confuse or mislead third parties regarding any relationship, association, or sponsorship with LViS.
  • On unauthorized products or services that may harm our reputation.
  • In any content that promotes illegal, discriminatory, violent, or offensive activities.
  • To register domains, social media profiles, or any other digital asset containing our brands or trade names without prior authorization.

4. Brand Presentation

To ensure consistency in our identity, any use of our brands must comply with the following guidelines:

  • Respect the colors, proportions, and typography specified in our Corporate Identity Manual.
  • Do not modify, distort, crop, or alter the brands in any way.
  • Do not overlay the brands with other graphics, text, or images that hinder their identification.

The Corporate Identity Manual is available to authorized partners and customers upon request.

5. Product References

The names and descriptions of the products and services offered by LViS must be used clearly and accurately. It is not permitted to:

  • Rename products or services to present them as one’s own.
  • Alter or misrepresent the main characteristics of products in presentations or descriptions.

6. Reporting Improper Use

If you detect improper use of our brands or products, please notify us via our email address info@lvis.es. We will investigate each case and take the necessary legal measures to protect our intellectual property.

7. Consequences of Unauthorized Use

Unauthorized use of our brands, trade names, or products may result in:

  • A demand for immediate cessation of the improper use.
  • Legal claims, including compensation for damages.
  • Suspension or termination of any contractual relationship with LViS.

8. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time to adapt to legal changes or business needs. Updated versions will be published on our website with the corresponding revision date.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding the use of our brands or products, you may contact us through:

Thank you for your cooperation in protecting and respecting our brands and products.
