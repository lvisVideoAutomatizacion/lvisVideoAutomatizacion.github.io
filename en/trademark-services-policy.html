LViS Brand and Product Policy

Last update: December 4, 2024.
At LViS, we value the integrity and recognition of our brands, products, and services. This policy aims to regulate the use of our trademarks, logos, trade names, and any other related assets, as well as establish guidelines for the proper representation of our products.

1. Ownership of Trademarks

All rights to the trademarks, logos, trade names, designs, and other identifying elements of LViS are the exclusive property of the company LViS Vídeo Automatización S.L. or its licensors. This includes but is not limited to:

  • The names "LViS Studio", "LViS Me", "LViS Starter", "LViS Actions Builder", "LViS Videogram Builder", and any other trade name associated with the platform.
  • Logos, slogans, brand images, icons, and graphic elements.

2. Permitted Use of Trademarks

The use of our trademarks is allowed only in the following cases:

  • Registered customers: To promote that they use our services, always respecting the established brand guidelines.
  • Authorized partners: Through a written agreement that permits the specific use of our trademarks.
  • Informative mentions: To reference LViS in articles, publications, or non-commercial content, provided it is accurate and not misleading.

3. Prohibited Use of Trademarks

The use of our trademarks is not allowed in the following cases:

  • To confuse or mislead third parties regarding the relationship, association, or sponsorship with LViS.
  • In unauthorized products or services that may damage our reputation.
  • In any content promoting illegal, discriminatory, violent, or offensive activities.
  • To register domains, social media profiles, or any other digital assets containing our trademarks or trade names without prior authorization.

4. Presentation of Trademarks

To ensure the consistency of our identity, any use of our trademarks must comply with the following guidelines:

  • Respect the colors, proportions, and fonts indicated in our Corporate Identity Manual.
  • Do not modify, distort, crop, or alter the trademarks in any way.
  • Do not overlay the trademarks with other graphics, texts, or images that hinder their identification.

The Corporate Identity Manual is available to authorized partners and clients upon request.

5. References to Products

The names and descriptions of the products and services offered by LViS must be used clearly and accurately. The following is not allowed:

  • Renaming products or services to present them as your own.
  • Altering or misrepresenting the main features of the products in presentations or descriptions.

6. Reporting Misuse

If you detect improper use of our trademarks or products, please notify us via email at info@lvis.es We will investigate each case and take the necessary legal actions to protect our intellectual property.

7. Consequences of Unauthorized Use

Unauthorized use of our trademarks, trade names, or products may result in:

  • A demand for the immediate cessation of improper use.
  • Legal claims, including compensation for damages.
  • Suspension or termination of any contractual relationship with LViS.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time to adapt to legal changes or business needs. Updated versions will be published on our website with the corresponding revision date.
