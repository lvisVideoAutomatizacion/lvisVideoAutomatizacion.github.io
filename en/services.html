Services

Complete Solutions for Making Your Videos Accessible

Audio Description

Description of images, actions, and important visual elements of the video through additional narration.

Closed Captions (CC)

Creation of accurate captions that include not only dialogues but also important sounds (effects, music, etc.).

Video Transcription

Complete transcription of the spoken content in the video, ideal for creating captions and facilitating review. Moreover, the text transcription can be converted to Braille.

How Does the Process Work?

Creating Accessible Videos is Easy with Us!
The process is fast, professional, and transparent. Here’s how we work:

1- Send Your Videos: Share the original videos and details about your needs (language, tone, etc.).

2- We Create and Review: We develop the audio description, captions, and transcription. We send the first draft for review.

3- Adjustments and Testing: We make adjustments based on your feedback and conduct tests on different devices.

4- Final Delivery: We deliver the accessible video along with caption, audio description, and transcription files. If desired, the transcription can be converted to Braille.
