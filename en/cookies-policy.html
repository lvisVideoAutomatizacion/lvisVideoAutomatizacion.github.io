LViS Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2024.
At LViS, we use cookies to improve your user experience, personalize content, and analyze the use of our platform. Below, we explain what cookies are, which types we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. These files allow information about your visit to be remembered, such as your preferences or login details, in order to offer a more personalized experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1. According to Their Purpose

  • Technical or Necessary Cookies
    • They allow the basic functioning of the platform, such as navigation and access to secure areas.
    • Example: Session identification, language settings.
  • Preference Cookies
    • They store your preferences to personalize the platform experience.
    • Example: Remembering user-selected settings.
  • Analytics Cookies
    • They collect anonymous information about how users interact with the platform. This helps us improve design and functionality.
    • Example: Number of visits, most popular pages.
  • Advertising Cookies
    • Used to show you personalized ads based on your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

2.2. According to Their Duration

  • Session Cookies
    • They are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies
    • They remain on your device for a defined period of time or until you manually delete them.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies used on our platform belong to third parties, such as analytics providers (for example, Google Analytics) or advertising services. These third parties may collect data about your activity on our platform and on other websites.

4. How to Manage Cookies

4.1. Browser Settings

  • You can configure your browser to:
    • Accept or reject all cookies.
    • Delete cookies already stored on your device.
    • Block cookies from specific websites.
  • Check your browser’s guides to manage cookies:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Safari
    • Microsoft Edge

4.2. Platform Settings

When you access our website for the first time, we will display a banner with options to accept, reject, or customize the cookies you wish to enable.

4.3. Withdrawal of Consent

At any time, you can modify your cookie preferences using the configuration link available on our platform.

5. Consequences of Disabling Cookies

Some platform functionalities may not be available or may not function correctly if you disable certain cookies, especially technical or necessary ones.

6. Updates to the Cookie Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in the use of cookies or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
