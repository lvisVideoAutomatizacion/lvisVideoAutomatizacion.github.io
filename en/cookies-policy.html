Cookie Policy of LViS

Last updated: December 4, 2024.
At LViS, we use cookies to enhance your user experience, personalize content, and analyze the use of our platform. Below, we explain what cookies are, the types we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. These files allow us to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or login details, to offer a more personalized experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1. By Purpose
  • Technical or Necessary Cookies
    • Enable the basic functioning of the platform, such as navigation and access to secure areas.
    • Example: Session identification, language settings.
  • Preference Cookies
    • Store your preferences to personalize your experience on the platform.
    • Example: Remembering user-selected settings.
  • Analytical Cookies
    • Collect anonymous information on how users interact with the platform. This helps us improve the design and functionality.
    • Example: Number of visits, most popular pages.
  • Advertising Cookies
    • Used to show you personalized ads based on your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
2.2. By Duration
  • Session Cookies
    • Automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies
    • Remain on your device for a defined time or until you manually delete them.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies used on our platform belong to third parties, such as analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) or advertising services. These third parties may collect data about your activity on our platform and other websites.

4. How to Manage Cookies

4.1. Browser Settings
  • You can configure your browser to:
    • Accept or reject all cookies.
    • Delete cookies already stored on your device.
    • Block cookies from specific websites.
  • Check your browser's guides to manage cookies:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Safari
    • Microsoft Edge
4.2. Platform Settings

When you access our website for the first time, we will display a banner with options to accept, reject, or customize the cookies you want to activate.

4.3. Withdrawal of Consent

You can modify your cookie preferences at any time using the settings link available on our platform.

5. Consequences of Disabling Cookies

Some functionalities of the platform may not be available or may not work correctly if you disable certain cookies, especially technical or necessary cookies.

6. Updates to the Cookie Policy

We may occasionally update this policy to reflect changes in the use of cookies or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
