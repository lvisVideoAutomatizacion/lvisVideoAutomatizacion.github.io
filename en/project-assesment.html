Audiovisual Accessibility Diagnosis

In one month, you will get a clear and actionable vision
of the accessibility status of your multimedia content,

  • • This is the first key step: You cannot move forward without knowing where you start.
  • • The analysis does not interfere with your operations You can do it without stopping anything. No integration or technical changes are required during the analysis.
  • • It allows you to justify resources and costs with real data to management or technical leads.
  • • Report detailing for each video typology the techniques to apply in multimedia production and publishing.

How the evaluation works

  • • Request an initial session to define the evaluation project.
  • • We prepare a tailored diagnosis proposal that includes:
    • • Estimation of multimedia volume based on elements, footage, and typologies.
    • • Selection of a representative sample for analysis and review. Within 15 days we complete the project analyzing up to 60 minutes in total or 10 different videos.
    • • Report with detected accessibility deviations in the sample and prioritized improvement recommendations.
  • • Includes an explanatory session at the end of up to two hours with your accessibility or communication team.
