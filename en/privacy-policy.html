The processing of your data is carried out in accordance with the following legal bases:

5. Disclosure of Data to Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service providers: Third parties acting as data processors, such as hosting providers, analytics tools, or payment service providers.
  • Legal obligations: Government authorities when required by law.
  • Corporate transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of all or part of assets, or any other corporate transaction involving a third party, we may share, disclose, or transfer user data to the successor entity (including during the pre-transaction phase).
  • Third parties after aggregation or anonymization: We may disclose or use aggregated or anonymized data (i.e., data that cannot be linked to an identified or identifiable natural person) for any purpose.

All third parties we work with guarantee adequate data protection and comply with the GDPR.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your data while your account remains active. Once the account is closed, data will be retained for a maximum period of 4 years in order to comply with our legal obligations or until you request its deletion, unless we are required to retain it for other legal reasons.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, unless we are required to retain it for legal reasons.
  • Object: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, such as commercial communications.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured and commonly used format, or request its transfer to another controller.
  • Restriction of processing: Temporarily restrict the use of your data.

To exercise your rights, please send a request to our email address dpo@lvis.es, specifying the right you wish to exercise.

Any request regarding rights will be resolved as soon as possible and, in any case, within the maximum period established by applicable legislation from the time it is received. In some cases, we may need to request a copy of your identity document or another form of identification in order to verify your identity.
