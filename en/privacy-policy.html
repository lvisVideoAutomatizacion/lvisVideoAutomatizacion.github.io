Mandatory information will be indicated with an asterisk or in a similar way. Without this information, we may not be able to address your inquiries or requests.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service providers: Third parties acting as data processors, such as hosting providers, analytics tools, or payment services.
  • Legal obligations: Government authorities as required by law.
  • Corporate transactions: In cases of mergers, acquisitions, or other corporate operations involving third parties.
  • Aggregated or anonymized data: We may disclose aggregated or anonymized data for any purpose.

All third parties we collaborate with guarantee adequate data protection and comply with GDPR.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your data as long as your account remains active. Once the account is closed, the data will be kept for a maximum period of 4 years to fulfill our legal obligations or until you request its deletion unless we are required to keep it for other legal reasons.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase: Request the deletion of your personal data unless we are required to retain it for legal reasons.
  • Object: Oppose the processing of your data for specific purposes, such as commercial communications.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured and commonly used format or request its transfer to another party.
  • Restrict processing: Temporarily limit the use of your data.

To exercise your rights, send a request to our email dpo@lvis.es, specifying the right you wish to exercise.

We will respond to any rights requests as soon as possible and, in any case, within the legally established maximum period from receipt. In some cases, we may need to request a copy of your ID or another identification document to verify your identity.
